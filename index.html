Howitdun?

  • Use the mouse to pick your weapons!
  • High Concept: The second player must guess the first player's actions!
  • Player Experience Goals: I want the first player to feel some stress relief and the second player to feel smart (if theywin, that is).
  • Genre: This game is kind of like a logic game. Not strategy, just logic.
  • Game play: Simply click which weapons you want to use or guess have been used.
  • Prototype goal: My first two-player game! Sort of.
  • Features: An act of murder! With the investigation part included.
  • Setting/Premise: Murderer: you have a grudge against this guy. Investigator: You know who did it, but how did they do it?
  • Story: (not much more to it than the setting above)
  • Target Audience: People into competative logic games. Kinda.
  • Play time: A minute or two. It takes longer for the investigator to take their turn than it does the murderer.
  • Strategies: Murderer: Think what would be challenging to decipher? Investigator: I'll give you a big hint. Bodies don't bleed much after death.
  • Prototype Assets: ALL SELF-MADE
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Simon?
